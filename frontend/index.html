The Problem

Most security systems watch but they don't warn. By the time an alarm is raised, the damage is done. They miss early cues: loitering, tension, pacing, possession of arms and panic.

Our Solution

EchoWatch is an AI-powered early warning system. It detects subtle threats before they explode into incidents. We analyze body language, sound spikes and movement patterns, giving security teams the edge to act in real time.

How It Works

Our system follows a simple yet powerful three-step process to provide proactive security.

1

Capture

EchoWatch seamlessly integrates with any existing camera feed, capturing high-resolution video and audio data from the environment in real-time.

2

Analyze

Our proprietary AI model, trained on thousands of hours of data, analyzes every frame for pre-threat indicators like nervous behavior, concealed objects, and aggressive tones.

3

Alert

The moment a potential threat is detected, the system issues an instant, multi-channel alert to security personnel, providing the context needed to act decisively.
